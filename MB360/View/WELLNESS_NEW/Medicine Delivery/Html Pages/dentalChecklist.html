A valid Prescription should contain Prescription date, Patient details, Doctor Detail & Dosage details.
Prescription should be on the letter head of the Doctor / Clinic / Hospital
Prescription must not be more than 1 year old and should be legible
Allopathy medicines prescribed by allopathy doctors are eligible.
Schedule H, X etc. drugs cannot be ordered online.
